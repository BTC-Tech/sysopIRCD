sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. SysOp Help & Information - AGLINE
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. Simillar to a network ban an AGLINE (auto gline) will
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. check the user address on connect against the AGLINE
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. database and if matched will gline the user from the
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. network with the reason specified. The time frame can
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. be w/d/h/m/s (weeks,days,hours,mins,secs) for example 1w or 7d
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. would ban them for 1 week, 1h would ban them for 1 hour
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. The reason field is optional and may be left blank.
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4.
sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. How To: /msg $netconf(system,nickname) AGLINE <nick/address> <time> <reason>

sockwrite -tn serv :SYSOP NOTICE $pnick :4,4. How To Remove: /msg $netconf(system,nickname) AGLINE REMOVE <ID>
